We at Pizza Knights know that a lot great memories are held around Pizza, whether it be a fun birthday party, a fun friday night after a game, or a great night at home making delicious Pizza, we know that you have some great stories to tell us that revolve around Pizza, and we want to hear them.

Powerbombed in the Pizzeria

I was once in Phil's Pizza on 202 in Branchburg, New Jersey. Highly recommended if anyone is wondering. Anyway, my friend who can be a bit loud was giving my other friend a hard time as he normally does. Nothing too mean here, everyone is friends and this is just how we are. However, that night we happened to be in the same place as a few professional wrestlers. There was an independent promotion going on in the middle school gymnasium. One of the dudes at the wrestlers table was the named the Pinnacle. He was huge and had wrestled on tv for a couple of years. The wrestler was a little tired from the show and kindly asked my friend th calm down. However, my friend showed him a little attitude and that was it. The wrestler got up, picked up my friend like he weighed nothing, and powerbombed him into a table. We waited a couple of weeks before we came back to that place.

Sting at the Pizza Joint

I was once in Il Forno with my friend and his family. The police busted into the place and arrested the Pizza chef for illegal Pizza smuggling, whatever that is. The big problem was that no one was left to make the Pizzas. Luckily, I'm somewhat of Pizza chef myself, and was able to take over for the night. I made Pizzas for our table and everyone else's, and even handled the takeout and delivery orders as well. Luckily, the Pizza chef was released after the whole episode was revealed to be a big misunderstanding. It was a memorable night for sure.
